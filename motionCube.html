Streamlined GLP level I.N.D. enabling packages

With pre-clinical animal model (rodent and primate) using high spatiotemporal resolution imaging and Deep Learning enabled biomarker quantifications, to complete I.N.D. applications with conventional in vivo PKPD & toxicity analysis

Translational Biomarker digitization

We re-construct and dense-caption the behavioral space of our animal model, element-by-element, using Deep Learning and NLP algorithms.

Data parcellation

Continuous motion input datasets are segmented using HMM or Deep Learning algorithms into distinct behavioral elements, which represent identical behaviors while capturing temporal variations. MotionCube integrates all these elements with dense captions, allowing elastic searches and ensemble comparisons across genotypes or SMW compound treatment.

Biomarker quantification

Based on spatiotemporal features extracted from comparable datasets, we measure disease model specific behavioral changes at various levels in an unprecedented way, such as gross motion (whole body level) or micro-motion (face recognition or hand gestures). Such detailed analysis provide the most sensitive and accurate measure of any relevant therapy, from small molecules to gene therapy, from microbiome to stem cell therapy.
